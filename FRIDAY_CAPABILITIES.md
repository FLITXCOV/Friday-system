# 🤖 F.R.I.D.A.Y. Capabilities Guide

Welcome to the F.R.I.D.A.Y. Operating System. She has been explicitly programmed to handle a specific set of tools securely and efficiently.

Here is a complete list of everything she can do for you:

## 🎵 1. Spotify Control
*   **Play Music:** Tell her to play a specific song, artist, album, or even one of your personal playlists (e.g. "Friday, play my Liked Songs on Spotify").
*   **Playback Control:** You can tell her to "pause", "resume", "skip to the next track", or "go back to the previous track".

## 🖥️ 2. Hardware Management
*   **Volume:** Tell her to change the volume (e.g. "Friday, set the volume to 50%").
*   **Brightness:** Tell her to adjust your screen brightness (e.g. "Friday, lower the brightness to 20%").

## 🗺️ 3. Maps & Media
*   **YouTube:** Ask her to search for something on YouTube or just open the main page (e.g. "Friday, search YouTube for Iron Man clips").
*   **Google Maps:** Ask her for directions. She will open Google Maps and calculate the driving route from your current location to your destination (e.g. "Friday, give me directions to New York City").
*   **Google Earth:** Ask her to locate a place on Google Earth (e.g. "Friday, show me the Eiffel Tower on Google Earth").
*   **Web Browsing:** Tell her to open any website (e.g. "Friday, open Instagram").

## 📱 4. WhatsApp & Screenshots
*   **Send Messages:** Ask her to send a text message to someone in your WhatsApp (e.g. "Friday, send a WhatsApp message to John saying I'll be late"). She will automatically search for their name and type the message.
*   **Send Screenshots:** Ask her to take a screenshot and send it to someone (e.g. "Friday, take a screenshot and send it to my friend in WhatsApp"). She will capture your current screen and attach it to the chat automatically.

## 🧠 5. Long-Term Memory
*   **Remember Facts:** Tell her to remember something about you, and she will save it permanently (e.g. "Friday, remember that my favorite color is red"). She will be able to recall it even after you reboot her.

## 🪟 6. Window Management
*   **Close Tabs:** If you have too many windows open, you can ask her to close the current tab (e.g. "Friday, close this tab"). 

## 💤 7. System Sleep
*   **Shutdown Protocol:** If you say the exact phrase **"Friday, system sleep"**, she will instantly terminate all of her background processes and shut herself down completely.
